Nabati Cheese Wafer – Creamy Layered Delight

A deliciously crunchy wafer layered with rich and creamy cheese filling. Perfectly balanced in flavor and texture, making it an irresistible anytime snack.

🧀 Ingredients

  • Wheat Flour
  • Vegetable Oil (Palm Oil)
  • Sugar
  • Cheese Powder
  • Milk Powder
  • Salt
  • Emulsifier (Soy Lecithin)
  • Flavor Enhancer
  • Permitted Food Additives & Coloring

⚠️ Allergen Information

Contains: Milk, Wheat, Soy

May contain traces of: Eggs, Nuts
