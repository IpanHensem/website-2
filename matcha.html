Homemade Matcha – Traditional Japanese Green Tea Powder

A vibrant and smooth green tea beverage made by whisking premium powdered matcha with hot water, delivering a rich umami flavor and natural sweetness.

🧀 Ingredients

  • Premium matcha green tea powder
  • Hot water
  • Sweetener (optional, e.g., honey or sugar)
  • Milk or milk alternative (optional for matcha latte)

⚠️ Allergen Information

Contains: None (unless milk or milk alternative is added)

🧾 Nutritional Facts (Per 240ml)

Energy5 kcal
Total Fat0 g
Saturated Fat0 g
Carbohydrates1 g
Sugars0 g
Protein0.3 g
Caffeine35 mg
Sodium0 mg

🕒 Shelf Life & Storage

  • Best consumed immediately after preparation.
  • Store matcha powder in a cool, dry, and dark place.

📦 Packaging Info

  • Typically sold in sealed tins or pouches to preserve freshness
  • Reusable containers recommended for storage

🌍 Sustainability

Made from sustainably grown tea leaves with eco-friendly packaging options encouraged.

📍 Product Origin

Traditionally sourced from Japan or other premium green tea producing regions
