Homemade Cappuccino Coffee – Classic Espresso with Frothy Milk

A rich and creamy cappuccino made from freshly brewed espresso coffee, steamed milk, and topped with a thick layer of milk foam for a smooth finish.

🧀 Ingredients

  • Freshly brewed espresso coffee
  • Steamed whole milk
  • Milk foam
  • Sugar (optional)
  • Cocoa powder or cinnamon for garnish (optional)

⚠️ Allergen Information

Contains: Milk

May contain traces of nuts if garnish is used.

🧾 Nutritional Facts (Per 180ml)

Energy110 kcal
Total Fat4.0 g
Saturated Fat2.5 g
Carbohydrates10.0 g
Sugars9.0 g
Protein5.0 g
Caffeine80 mg
Sodium85 mg

🕒 Shelf Life & Storage

  • Best consumed immediately after preparation.
  • Store refrigerated if kept and consume within 24 hours.

📦 Packaging Info

  • Served fresh in cups or mugs
  • Reusable cups encouraged for sustainability

🌍 Sustainability

Prepared with sustainably sourced coffee beans and encourages reusable cups to reduce waste.

📍 Product Origin

Prepared fresh at home or coffee shop
