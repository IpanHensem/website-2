Seasons Soy Milk – Nutritious Plant-Based Drink

A smooth and creamy soy milk drink made from high-quality soybeans, offering a healthy alternative to dairy milk.

  • Water
  • Soybeans
  • Sugar
  • Calcium Carbonate
  • Vitamin D
  • Natural Flavourings
