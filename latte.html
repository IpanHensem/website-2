Homemade Latte Coffee – Creamy and Rich Coffee Delight

A smooth and creamy homemade latte, made with freshly brewed espresso and steamed milk, perfect for coffee lovers seeking a rich and comforting beverage.

🧀 Ingredients

  • Freshly brewed espresso coffee
  • Steamed whole milk
  • Milk foam
  • Sugar (optional)
  • Flavorings (vanilla, caramel – optional)

⚠️ Allergen Information

Contains: Milk

May contain traces of nuts if flavored syrups are used.

🧾 Nutritional Facts (Per 250ml)

Energy120 kcal
Total Fat4.5 g
Saturated Fat2.7 g
Carbohydrates12.0 g
Sugars11.0 g
Protein6.0 g
Caffeine80 mg
Sodium90 mg

🕒 Shelf Life & Storage

  • Best consumed immediately after preparation.
  • Keep refrigerated if stored and consume within 24 hours.

📦 Packaging Info

  • Served fresh in cups or mugs
  • Reusable cups recommended for sustainability

🌍 Sustainability

Homemade with care, supporting sustainable coffee sourcing and reducing single-use waste by encouraging reusable containers.

📍 Product Origin

Prepared fresh at home or coffee shop
